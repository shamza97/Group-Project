LA PARILLA

OUR HISTORY

La Parilla was created following the encounter of the two creators in 2019 during a coding class. Sola was studying Law at the time and Axelle was studying International Business, both final years. They decided one day, to give up on their studies and become their own bosses. Within that same year, they managed to open their first restaurant which grew successful organically in the vibrant city that is Manchester.

OUR VISION AND MISSION

Our mission: To inspire others through the traditional Mexican cuisine. This quality Cuisine is a full-fledged experience. Made to the highest standard with the best ingredients by our specially trained chefs.
